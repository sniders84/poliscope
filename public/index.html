Rank Official Office Value

Rankings Scoring and Weight Logic

Officials are ranked using weighted contributions from legislative and executive activity. Each category has a raw value, a weight, and a contribution (raw × weight). The sum forms the Power Score.

Legislative Offices (Senators & Representatives)

CategoryWeightRationale
Bills Sponsored1.2Shows initiative and legislative drive.
Bills Cosponsored0.6Collaboration matters, but less than leading bills.
Bills Enacted (Sponsored)6.0Turning proposals into law.
Bills Enacted (Cosponsored)3.0Influence through cosponsored bills.
Committee Memberships4.0Committees drive legislation.
Committee Leadership Roles2.0Leadership positions add influence.
Missed Votes-0.5Penalizes absenteeism.
